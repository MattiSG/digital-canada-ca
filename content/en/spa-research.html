Help us make it easier to find opportunities to sell your goods or services to governments

We’re conducting research to try to improve how businesses find opportunities to work with the public sector in Canada. 

The Canadian Digital Service and Public Services and Procurement Canada are trying to learn more about the experiences of businesses looking for government opportunities. We’d like to understand what makes a good procurement opportunity for you, and what information you need to decide to respond.

If you participate, for a period of one week, you’ll be asked to: 

  • reflect on your daily activities related to public sector procurement 
  • report your experiences by email. 

Right now, we’re just asking for some basic information about your business to help us identify a diverse group of participants. 

By participating, you will help make it easier for businesses like yours to find relevant opportunities to respond to.

What we will collect

By emailing us, you consent that the following information will be collected: name, email address, legal name of business, size of business (number of full-time employees), location of business, nature of business ownership, and whether or not you have previous experience in public sector procurement.

How we will use this information

We work with the Canadian Digital Service (CDS). We are working with Public Service and Procurement Canada (PSPC) to improve how businesses find contracts with governments. 

To do this we will ask you to provide us with some information about your business. We collect this information to ensure our research cohorts are diverse.

Your participation and answers will not affect your access to Government of Canada services or benefits.

Your contact information may be shared with other researchers at CDS and PSPC, for the purpose of inviting you to participate in future research activities related to improving public sector procurement. You may withdraw your information at any time by contacting the researcher listed above. 

Your information will be confidential. This means it will not be linked to you in any public reports.

This personal information will not be used for any “administrative purposes”. Your information will not be used to make a decision that affects you or your access to Government of Canada services.

Who we are

CDS is part of the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat (TBS).

  • The collection and use of your personal information by TBS is authorized by the Financial Administration Act.

  • The collection and use of your personal information by PSPC is authorized by the Privacy Act.

  • The collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information is in accordance with the federal Privacy Act. Under the Privacy Act, you have a right of protection, access to and correction or notation of your personal information.
